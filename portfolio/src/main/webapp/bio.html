My Story

I am a British Born Nigerian currently studying Computer Science at Howard University. My parents are Nigerian from the Yoruba tribe and I was born in London. I moved to Nigeria in 2007 where I completed my elementary school. I was admitted into Loyola Jesuit College, a prestigious boardiing school in Nigeria, where I studied until 2017 when I graduated and moved to the United States where I began at Howard University.
